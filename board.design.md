# Owela Board Design

## Board Structure
- Total holes: 60
- Player 1: 30 holes
- Player 2: 30 holes
- Players face each other.
- Movement direction: counter-clockwise.

## Starting Position
Each player has:
- 30 holes
- 19 holes containing seeds
- 11 empty holes
- Each filled hole starts with 2 seeds

## Seed Layout
- 15 filled holes in the main row
- 4 filled holes in the inner row
- The 4 inner-row filled holes are positioned in the middle area.

## Movement
- Players choose a hole on their side.
- Seeds are distributed one by one.
- If the last seed lands in an occupied hole, the player continues sowing.
- The turn ends when the last seed lands in an empty hole.

## Capturing
- A capture happens when the final seed lands in an occupied inner-row hole.
- Opposite opponent seeds can be captured.

## Winning
- The game ends when a player cannot make a move or has no seeds.
- The player with the most captured seeds wins.
