# Owela Board Layout

## Overview
The Owela board has:
- 60 holes total
- 30 holes for Player 1
- 30 holes for Player 2
- Two players face each other

## Player Positions
- Player 1 sits on one side of the board (sunrise side)
- Player 2 sits opposite (sunset side)
- Each player sees the board from their own direction

## Hole Arrangement
Each player has:
- 15 holes in the outer row
- 15 holes in the inner row
- 19 starting holes containing seeds
- 11 empty holes at the beginning

## Starting Seeds
- Each filled hole contains 2 seeds
- Seed positions:
  - 15 filled holes in one row
  - 4 filled holes in the inner row
  - The 4 inner-row holes are positioned in the middle area

## Movement
- Players sow seeds counter-clockwise
- The board must support movement from both player viewpoints

## Future App Design
The digital board will include:
- Touch controls
